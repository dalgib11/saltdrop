About


Tired of hauling heavy salt bags? Saltdrop delivers premium water softener salts straight to your home—on your terms. Choose doorstep delivery for a quick drop-off, or let our team carry it in, down the stairs, and right to your softener unit. We’ll even top off your salt barrel, so you don’t have to lift a finger. No stress, no strain—just clean, softened water made easy.










How It Works:


Getting soft water delivered to your home is simple. Just fill out the form at the bottom of the page, select the delivery package that suits your needs, and choose between convenient doorstep dropoff or In-Home Delivery. Once we receive your request, we’ll contact you to schedule a delivery time that works best for you.








  • 1. Select Your Delivery Package

  • 2. Submit Your Details
